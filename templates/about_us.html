Why a website on Zero Waste?


Zero Waste is a demonstrative website run by Najma, Rachel and Sophie to demonstrate the growing problem of global waste and how we can minimise it to create a better world for future generations. We dedicate our website to highlighing the dangers of climate change and how using more sustanable measures can create a better world.

Why is tackling waste so important?


In displaying content of various zero waste ideas, we intend to use our website as part of an initiative to inspire people of today and tomorrow that saving the planet one step at a time is possible. We want people to understand that they are the future of society and every person is capable of achieving success by doing their part. As an online community, we welcome the work and voices of emerging enviromentalists by providing a platform where anyone – from professionals to young people – can contribute to this platform through the Join the Community page.

This website is constructed as part of the Code: First Girls Course Competition, Advanced Python course in 2019.Code First: Girls is a multi-award winning social enterprise that works to help increase diversity in tech by teaching young women and non-binary people how to code.
